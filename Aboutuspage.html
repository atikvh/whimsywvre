About us

Welcome to WhimsyWare Co., where creativity meets compassion. At WhimsyWare, we take pride in crafting exquisite stained glassware and a variety of other glasswares that not only enhance your living space but also reflect our commitment to environmental sustainability. Our pieces are meticulously designed to bring whimsical beauty into your surroundings while promoting eco-friendly practices. What sets us apart is our dedication to making a positive impact beyond artistry. We are proud to announce that 50% of our profits go directly to supporting the people of Gaza, demonstrating our solidarity with Palestine and our commitment to raising awareness to halt the ongoing genocide. With WhimsyWare Co., your purchase not only adds a touch of elegance to your home but also contributes to a cause that truly matters. Join us in spreading love, awareness, and supporting those in need.
